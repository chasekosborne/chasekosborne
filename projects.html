Filming the Total Solar Eclipse
Before the total solar eclipse, a couple of friends from college and I put together a weather balloon to catch some footage of the eclipse from space. We put together and sent up a weather balloon along with a GoPro and radio transmitter, to our surprise, we were able to track and find the Payload, from that we got this footage and telemetary data which you can view here.
